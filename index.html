Join the Mission to Make Housing Affordable

Affordable Housing = Empowerment

We believe affordable housing is an important step towards community empowerment and sustainable economic development. We work with experienced professionals to buy and upgrade vacant housing to Federal HUD standards. Our finished homes are finally sold to qualified first time home buyers with a subsidy to maintain affordability for 5, 10 or 15 years.
